About.

I’m William Huang, an undergraduate at Stanford studying Computer Science and Physics. For a summary of my professional education and experience, my resume is located here. If you’d like to contact me, please email willsh@stanford.edu.

I’m passionate about all things computer science and physics. To be more specific, on the computer science side, I’m interested in the multitude of applications of artificial intelligence and machine learning to develop better solutions to problems on the personal, communal, industrial, and global scales. On the physics side, I have a fascination of all things electrical and quantum, and I aspire to harness the power of electrical engineering and the potential of quantum computing to advance technology and society.
